On this page

Note

To even begin accessing internal resources you must be added to the Bellevue College InfoSec SSO list, if you are not added, you can request to be added by filling out this form or by attending a meeting in-person.

Unfortunately this process is not automated, as such, please give up to a week (during active quarters).

If the process is taking to long reach out to: infosec@bellevuecollege.edu

Accessing Internal Webpages

Currently the only accessible webpages to the open internet are:

  • Our CTF platform: ctf.bcinfosec.club
  • Our instance of Juice Shop: juice.bcinfosec.club
